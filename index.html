About

I lead teams to create intuitive, user-focused digital experiences blending aesthetics and functionality.

Background

Rooted in Advertising,
Driven by Data

Before transitioning into product design, I spent years in the advertising industry crafting campaigns and brand experiences for some of the world's most recognizable names. That foundation in storytelling, user empathy, and visual communication informs everything I build today.

From concept development and art direction to full-scale campaign launches, I led creative work across entertainment, streaming, and consumer brands. But I never relied on instinct alone. I consistently leaned on performance data, audience insights, and strategic frameworks to shape creative decisions, ensuring the work wasn't just beautiful but measurably effective. Across every team I've been on, I was the go-to person for digital innovation, always pushing to find new formats, platforms, and technologies before they became industry standard.

Case Study

Johnson & Johnson
PSC Command Center

Designed the "Golden Thread" framework for Johnson & Johnson's Patient Support Center, a unified command center dashboard connecting patient journeys, operational workflows, and real-time analytics into a single product experience. Led product UI and strategy across a 12-week engagement, translating complex healthcare data into clear, actionable interfaces.

Conducted stakeholder research, mapped end-to-end patient journeys, and delivered a modular dashboard system that gave leadership visibility into every stage of the support process, from intake through resolution.
